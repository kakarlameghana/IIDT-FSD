Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is known for its art, fashion, and culture. The iconic Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum are must-visit attractions.

  • Famous landmarks: Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral
  • Cuisine: Croissants, baguettes, macarons
  • Language spoken: French

Tokyo

Tokyo, the capital of Japan, is a bustling metropolis known for its futuristic skyscrapers, vibrant nightlife, and delicious cuisine.

  • Famous landmarks: Tokyo Tower, Shibuya Crossing, Meiji Shrine
  • Cuisine: Sushi, ramen, tempura
  • Language spoken: Japanese

Rome

Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city steeped in history and ancient architecture. The Colosseum and Vatican City are among its most famous landmarks.

  • Famous landmarks: Colosseum, Vatican City, Pantheon
  • Cuisine: Pasta, pizza, gelato
  • Language spoken: Italian
